About Me

Hello/Salam!

I'm Memuna an aspiring Human-Computer Interaction (HCI) researcher and User Experience (UX) designer from Lahore, Pakistan. Currently, I am a Master of Science in Information (MSI) student at the University of Michigan in Ann Arbor. I completed my undergraduate studies with a distinction in Computer Science and Psychology at the Lahore University of Management Sciences (LUMS). My aim is to develop equitable and inclusive, education and health technologies for marginalized and underrepresented communities around the world, especially in the Global South, where I hail from.
